Our Vision

With the recent pandemic interrupting the education of millions of students throughout the country, colleges and universities are struggling to find ways to let students back on campus. Thatâ€™s where Covidiena comes in. Our platform aims at easing the transition to in-person learning for college students, staff, and faculty during the ongoing crisis so students can continue their education in a stable and safe environment.

What We Do

Symptom Tracking healing

Students are encouraged to report their daily temperature and any other symptoms they might be experiencing. This allows the college's administration to monitor any possible cases of Covid19 on campus, and react appropriately.

Event Tracking event

All official on-campus events are submitted to Covidiena, where students, staff, and faculty can browse through events, their health/safety regulations, and maximum capacity.
